# Vendor Onboarding Guide — WedPlan Platform

> This document outlines the complete vendor onboarding flow. Share it with new vendors after registration.

---

## Overview

Welcome to **WedPlan Vendor Portal**! This guide walks you through the steps to get your business listed and accepting bookings from couples on the platform.

**Typical timeline:** 2–5 business days from registration to going live.

---

## Onboarding Checklist

### Step 1 — Create Your Account ✅
- [ ] Visit `/vendor-register` and complete the account form
- [ ] Enter your **business email** and a secure password (min. 8 characters)
- [ ] Provide your **phone number** for verification contact
- [ ] Submit the registration form

**What happens next:** You'll receive an email confirmation. Our team will begin the review process within 1 business day.

---

### Step 2 — Complete Your Business Profile
- [ ] Fill in your **business / brand name** (exactly as registered)
- [ ] Select the correct **service category** (e.g. Photography, Catering, Venue)
- [ ] Write a compelling **description** (min. 30 characters; we recommend 150–300)
- [ ] Add your **primary location** and service area
- [ ] Include your **website URL** if available
- [ ] Specify **years in business**

**Tips:**
- Use specific, keyword-rich descriptions to improve discoverability
- Mention the regions you serve (e.g. "Island-wide Sri Lanka")
- Highlight awards, certifications, or signature style

---

### Step 3 — Upload Documents & Portfolio
- [ ] Upload your **Business Logo** (JPG/PNG, max 3 MB) — appears on your profile card
- [ ] Upload **up to 5 portfolio images** showcasing your best work (max 5 MB each)
- [ ] Upload your **Business Registration Certificate** (PDF, JPG, or PNG, max 10 MB)
- [ ] Enter your **Business Registration Number** (e.g. PV12345)
- [ ] Enter your **Tax ID / VAT Number** (optional, but recommended)

**Document requirements:**
| Document | Format | Max Size | Required? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Business Registration Certificate | PDF, JPG, PNG, WEBP | 10 MB | ✅ Required |
| Business Logo | JPG, PNG, WEBP | 3 MB | Optional |
| Portfolio Images (up to 5) | JPG, PNG, WEBP | 5 MB each | Optional |

> 🔒 All uploaded documents are encrypted and used only for identity verification. They are **not** visible to couples.

---

### Step 4 — Set Your Pricing & Packages
- [ ] Enter your **starting price** (the lowest entry-level price you offer)
- [ ] Select the correct **currency** (LKR, USD, EUR, etc.)
- [ ] Add optional **pricing notes** (e.g. "Prices vary by date and headcount")
- [ ] Create up to **5 service packages** with name, price, and description

**Package examples:**
```
Essential Package — LKR 75,000
  Half-day coverage, 200+ edited photos

Signature Package — LKR 150,000
  Full day coverage, 400+ edited photos + highlight reel

Luxury Package — LKR 250,000
  Full day + pre-wedding shoot + cinematic film + slideshow
```

---

### Step 5 — Review & Submit Application
- [ ] Review all submitted information on the summary screen
- [ ] Confirm accuracy of business details, pricing, and documents
- [ ] Accept the **Terms of Service** and **Vendor Policy**
- [ ] Click **Submit Application**

---

## After Submission

### What Happens During Review?

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| ⏳ Pending Review | Application received, awaiting admin review |
| 🔍 Under Review | Admin is verifying your documents |
| ✅ Documents Verified | Documents confirmed, profile being activated |
| 🌐 Live | Profile is live and searchable by couples |
| ❌ Rejected | Application rejected (see admin notes for reason) |

### Timeline
- **Day 1–2:** Application received and queued for review
- **Day 2–3:** Documents verified and business information checked
- **Day 3–5:** Profile approved and made live (or rejection email sent)

---

## After Approval — Complete Your Profile

Once approved, log in to the **Vendor Portal** (`/vendor`) to:

- [ ] Add additional portfolio images
- [ ] Enable or disable specific service packages
- [ ] Set your **availability calendar** (block out booked dates)
- [ ] Respond to **booking enquiries** from couples
- [ ] View **analytics** — profile views, enquiry rate, conversion

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does approval take?
Typically 2–3 business days. You'll receive an email once reviewed.

### Can I update my profile after submission?
Yes! Once approved, log in to `/vendor` to update your profile, photos, and pricing at any time.

### What if my application is rejected?
You'll receive an email explaining the reason. Common reasons include:
- Missing or unclear business registration document
- Insufficient portfolio images
- Description too short or generic

You may reapply after addressing the noted issues.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. Documents are encrypted at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. No sensitive documents are shared publicly.

### Who can see my vendor profile?
Only your public-facing business name, description, category, location, pricing, and portfolio images are visible to couples. Personal contact details and documents remain private.
